Fallen Keep

Fallen Keep is an action-adventure dungeon crawler, in which the player—A magical knight–traverses through a cavernous stronghold. You find a plethora of globs, skeletons, and chests filled with treasure. As you make your way through the dungeon, you will gather keys to unlock the pathway.

OUR MISSION

At Spire Studios, we believe in the creation of video games that not only grow our skills as developers, but will provide players with an entertaining experience.

  • Working as a Team: We value working on new ideas and providing feedback as a team to gather everyone’s input in our creation.
  • The Process of Development: We value the development process behind making a video game, and have continued to improve on our skills and abilities to do with the procedure of doing so.
